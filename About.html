TelescopicTour

TelescopicTour is an American aerospace company dedicated to revolutionizing the aerospace industry and making affordable spaceflight a reality. Our company was founded with the goal of making the excitement and wonder of space accessible to anyone with the desire to explore. With TelescopicTour, you can choose from a variety of space-based travel packages, including suborbital “hot-air balloon” flights, rocket launches, and visits to the International Space Station. We are committed to ensuring the safety and comfort of all of our guests and have implemented rigorous training and safety protocols to ensure that every mission is a success. Whether you are a seasoned space enthusiast or just looking for a once-in-a-lifetime adventure, TelescopicTour has something for you. We invite you to join us on this incredible journey of discovery and exploration.
